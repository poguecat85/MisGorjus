Bio

MisGorjus is the first username I came up with from MySpace. I looked at myself, my pictures, and came up with a name that described me inside and out. Gorgeous! So I simplified a descriptive word and made it my own.

I was born Feb. 7, 1984.

I am a woman of many gifts, and a kind good-hearted person.

I love speaking positive in people's lives especially young women. I have so much to offer to the world, weather its through my smile, my words, my dance any of my gifts. I'm here to inspire someone. I'm here to say, "Stand up and go after you dreams, nothing is impossible!" I'm here to say, "Look past my face, and feel my heart beat through my chest. I've listened to friends and family for so long, and never have really had a voice outwardly, all the inside I'm dying to let this BIG voice be heard. I laugh, talk, chill, party-anyone will tell you that has had the opportunity of really getting to know me that, that I am a really cool person. Deep down inside though, MisGorjus isn't just cook, she is a diamond that was found dusty and dirty, but now, well polished and ready to shine bright for the world to see. It's not about the diamond, but the story of what made this diamond shine so bright.
